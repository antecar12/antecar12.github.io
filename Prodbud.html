Prodbud is a service where you as a musician can give life to songs and ideas by letting us produce your songs for you. If you are unfamiliar with the recording process, we offer a professional, high end-studio-grade production of your music for a very affordable price. We help local artists make their dreams of having their own recorded music tracks come true.

We will: Record (With the use of high end equipment and well mixed samples and sound libraries to fit the style, sound you are looking for.), mix (Compression, Reverb, Delay, Auto-tune etc) and master (Instruments, Backing-tracks, Levels, Frequencies etc.) your songs to perfection.
